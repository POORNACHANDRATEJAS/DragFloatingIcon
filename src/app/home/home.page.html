HOW IT WORKS?

Learn to Swim

Our swimming lessons are located in a superb 25m pool at West View Leisure Centre. Our team of enthusiastic ASA certified teachers are committed to ensuring your child reaches their maximum potential using our progressive swimming pathway scheme.

Competitive Squads

From the first steps in competitive swimming with our “D-squad”, through to national champions in our “Top Squad”, there is a training group to suit all swimmers. The competitive swimming side of the Club is organised into three basic sections.

About Water Polo

Water Polo is a seven-a-side team game played by swimmers in a pool, and consists of four quarters of eight minutes, in which the two teams attempt to score goals by throwing the ball into their opponent’s goal.
